Concept

Beats&Bites is a new concept scheduled to launch in summer 2020. Bordering the North Sea on the beach of Terschelling, we provide the very best in terms of food and music in our new beach club.

Visit us during the daytime to enjoy our meticulously prepared fresh and unique meals, or have a cocktail and chill out in our lounge area.

We periodically host parties in our own Beats&Bites-fashion: musically diverse, but with an emphasis on being funky and banging.

Visit our webshop and support our brand by buying vinyl releases from our record label, or share our philosophy by wearing our merchandise.

Drop by, spread the love and keep groovin'!
